About the Project

Why Supporting Research Matters

Deploying AI in clinical settings is not merely a technical problem. It demands rigorous evaluation of safety, fairness, workflow integration, and trust — the infrastructure that makes AI work for patients and clinicians.

Multi-Layer Evaluation

We evaluate AI systems across technical performance, clinical validity, and real-world deployment conditions to surface risks invisible in benchmark testing.

Longitudinal Studies

Short-term accuracy does not predict long-term reliability. Our studies track model behaviour over time and across patient populations.

Stakeholder Integration

Clinicians, patients, and administrators co-design our research protocols, ensuring findings translate to actionable practice change.

Safety First

Every recommendation is grounded in evidence of harm prevention. We maintain a living registry of known failure modes in deployed healthcare AI.

Key Findings

What the Research Reveals

The TIPOCA Framework

A Five-Pillar Approach to AI Operationalization

Based on our findings, we developed a structured framework that healthcare organizations can use to evaluate, deploy, and monitor AI systems responsibly.

01

Technical Validity

Rigorous evaluation of model performance across diverse, representative datasets before any clinical use.

Implementation

02

Workflow integration, EHR compatibility, alert fatigue mitigation, and clinician interface design.

03

Patient Outcomes

Direct measurement of patient safety metrics, care quality, and experience — not proxy metrics.

04

Operational Continuity

Ongoing monitoring, retraining pipelines, and governance structures to maintain performance over time.

05

Clinician Adoption

Trust-building, transparency mechanisms, training, and change management for sustainable use.
